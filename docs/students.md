# Students Manual

Welcome to grAIder!

As a student, grAIder acts as a tool to evaluate your code quality and verify that your coursework submissions meet the expectations of your course.

## How it works

1. **Submission**: You push your coursework (e.g. `swegl-fs26` projects) to your GitLab repository as instructed.
2. **Evaluation**: Your teachers will use grAIder to run `qlty` tools, tests, and coverage over your projects.
3. **AI Review**: In the future, grAIder's agentic AI review will provide detailed feedback based on your course's project brief (e.g., Modul-Projekt Home-Automation).
4. **Reports**: You receive a comprehensive grading report highlighting strengths and areas for improvement.

## Local setup
Ensure that your projects follow the required structure and you have pushed everything, including necessary `.qlty` or `.gitlab-ci.yml` configurations required for the evaluation.
